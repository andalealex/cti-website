Threat Trends

Threat trending is the process of modeling organization's threat landscapes through a global and local landscape through the use of industry reports from organizations like SANS, Verizon, FireEye, etc.The Global Threat Landscape looks at the greater, global industry whereas the Local Landscape looks at specific organizations.

Some of the questions being asked during the identification of threats are around understanding the number of breached companies, what's being impacted, how they're being attacked, what the organizational spend on security is, and the trend of attacks against the organization.

Aerospace & Defense Threat Trends

Taking a closer look at the A&D industry, we've identified the following threat trends:

  • State Sponsored Actors

  • Malware & Ransomware

  • Data Breaches

  • Third- Party & Supply Chain Attacks

  • Insider Attacks & Espionage (seeking proprietary information)
