Throughout the course of this MIS 562 CTI project, the group will be further examining the Aerospace & Defense industry.

Cyber Threat Intelligence, or CTI, is the systematic process of identifying emerging threats and key actors to enable effective decision making.

Characteristics of Good CTI

  • Relevant, timely
  • Actionable
  • Comprehensive
  • Helps organizations forecast and plan for the future
  • Must be created in a systematic, repeatable and efficient manner

Some examples of Major CTI Companies are FireEye, RSA Firstwatch, Cyveillance, and iSight.
