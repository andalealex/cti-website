Background of Industry

The Aerospace Defense Industry is a large portion of the private side of the defense industrial base for the United States. This industry has an annualized revenue of $169.4BN and a profit of $9BN per year. The industry has been on decline at 5.8% over the past five years, and is expected to rise at an annualized rate of 9% over the next five years.

The Department of Defense relies heavily on the A&D industry to provide them with the most technologically advanced and capable equipment on and off the battlefield. Without the robust A&D industry, the US DoD would not have the capability to maintain the global presence that it does.
